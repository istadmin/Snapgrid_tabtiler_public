# Privacy Policy — SnapGrid — Tab Tiler

**Effective date:** March 21, 2026
**Last updated:** March 21, 2026

## Introduction

SnapGrid — Tab Tiler ("SnapGrid", "the Extension") is a browser extension that arranges tabs and windows into grid layouts. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles user data.

## Data Collection

SnapGrid does **not** collect, store, process, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information.

Specifically, the Extension:

- Does **not** collect personally identifiable information (PII)
- Does **not** track, record, or monitor browsing history or activity
- Does **not** use analytics, telemetry, cookies, or tracking technologies
- Does **not** communicate with any external servers or third-party services
- Does **not** store any data locally beyond the current browser session
- Does **not** access the content of any web page

## Permissions

The Extension requires the following browser permissions, each used solely for its stated purpose:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `tabs` | Read tab titles and identifiers to display the tab picker interface. The Extension does not read or access tab URLs, page content, or browsing history. |
| `system.display` | Detect connected monitor dimensions and positions to accurately tile windows on multi-monitor setups. |
| `contextMenus` | Register right-click menu entries on the extension icon to provide quick access to layout and merge actions. |

## Data Storage

The Extension does not use browser local storage, session storage, IndexedDB, cookies, or any other persistent storage mechanism. All operations are performed in-memory and no data persists after the browser session ends.

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party service, API, or server.

## Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party under any circumstances, because no data is collected.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in this document with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
